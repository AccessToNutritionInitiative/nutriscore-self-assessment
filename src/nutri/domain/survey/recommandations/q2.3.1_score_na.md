question_text: "Based on the geographies you serve, do you make different products adapted to different needs?"
score: "NA"
---

Adapting product compositions, packaging sizes, and labelling to reflect local needs (e.g., micronutrient deficiencies or high rates of overweight/obesity) can help consumers make healthier choices, improve nutritional outcomes, and can help your business to increase its sales.

Specific target groups and geographies that you can adapt your products for include, but are not limited to:
o Populations living in Urban or Rural regions or different Provinces, States and Districts
o Target groups such as Infants, Children 1-3, Children 3-10, Adolescents 11-18, Adults, Elderly, Women of child-bearing age or those from lower-Income, Middle-income or Higher-income groups.
