We suggest you put a mechanism in place to receive feedback from your customers. All feedback, supportive and critical, can be helpful to improve your business! If you lack the capacity to respond to customers daily, consider setting up a monthly review. For recommendations on how to move forward, please contact the SUN Business Network.
