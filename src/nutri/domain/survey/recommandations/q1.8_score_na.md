Health might not yet be the most important factor for your business when considering product formulation. However, it is important to be aware that nutritious foods can improve the overall health of your local population, and that the demand for healthy food varieties is rising. The COVID-19 crises has amplified consumer awareness of the role healthy food plays in people’s resilience towards disease. There is high demand for natural, organic, simple, and healthy foods. Your company can both contribute to better consumer awareness and benefit from consumer demand with increased sales by making health one of the central considerations in your company’s innovation, R&D and product development strategies.
