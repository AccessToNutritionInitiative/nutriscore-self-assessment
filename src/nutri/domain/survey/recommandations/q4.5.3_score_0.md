Ensuring that any claims used on product labels is based on  relevant and sound science is of high important, please make sure you verify your claims by using studies, scientific literature reviews,  dietary guidelines  or on product composition data.
For more information, please look at Codex guidelines on health and nutrition claims:
<https://www.fao.org/ag/humannutrition/32444-09f5545b8abe9a0c3baf01a4502ac36e4.pdf>
