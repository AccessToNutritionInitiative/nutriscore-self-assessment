Being aware of, and following, national and/or international guidelines and standards such as the Health Star Rating (HSR), Nutri-score and WHO Nutrient Profiling models can help you to develop healthier products. Producing and selling healthier products can help influence consumers to make more health-conscious choices and can potentially help you to avoid losing business when consumers seek out healthier diets.
