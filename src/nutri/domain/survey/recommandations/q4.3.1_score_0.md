question_text: "Does your package have information on its shelf life and/or storage conditions?"
score: "0"
---

Please provide package information on storage/shelf life of products. Codex guidance on labelling also includes date marking and storage instructions asking to indicate a date of minimum durability. In addition to the date of minimum durability, any special conditions for the storage of the food shall be declared on the label if the validity of the date depends thereon.

<https://www.fao.org/3/y2770e/y2770e02.htm#:~:text=CODEX%20GENERAL%20STANDARD%20FOR%20THE%20LABELLING%20OF%20PREPACKAGED%20FOODS,-CODEX%20STAN%201&text=For%20the%20purpose%20of%20this,composition%20or%20any%20other%20quality>.
