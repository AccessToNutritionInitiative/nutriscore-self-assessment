category: 2
question: "2.4.1"
question_text: "Are you aware of the nutritional needs of the groups that you target?"
score: "5"
---

We encourage you to verify the information that you have on the needs of the population groups that you target with your products. You can do this by asking for information from your local health authority, government, civil society organizations or international agencies or online on accredited organizations websites.

We urge you to spread this information during your communication with other companies, particularly information regarding population groups that are at greater risk of malnutrition.
