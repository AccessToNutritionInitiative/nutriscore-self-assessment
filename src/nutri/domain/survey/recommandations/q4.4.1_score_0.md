Please put a system in place for quality control.

Implementing a system to ensure quality control throughout your business supply chain can improve the efficiency of processes in your company and increases the safety of products for consumption.
If food safety hazards are controlled for, companies may obtain a quality assurance certification or mark that can reduce risks of product withdrawal and provide reassurance to consumers and regulatory bodies.  In addition, certifications can improve chance that your products are accepted in different markets and reduce barriers to international trade.

Food safety management system certifications such as the ISO 22000: 2018 define what an organization should do to exhibit its ability to control food safety hazards and make sure that food is safe for consumption.

A food safety management system is based on seven principles of HACCP, which help your business to identify and control hazards before they threaten the safety of your food and customers.
To read more on importance of implementing a system for quality control, and what you should do to show your ability to control food safety hazards and make sure that food is safe for consumption, go to ISO 22000:2018 website here: <https://maarg.in/iso-22000-2018-certification-kerala/>
