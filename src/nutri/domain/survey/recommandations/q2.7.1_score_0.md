Please find out about the existing regulations in your country. Regulations and standards are designed to protect consumers by clearly setting out the ‘dos and don’ts’ for responsible marketing to ensure legal, honest, decent and truthful communications and practices, and they also provide guidance for companies. The most known standard for advertising self-regulation is the International Chamber of Commerce’s Advertising and Marketing Communications Code – or short: the ICC Marketing Code. This Marketing Code sets high standards for a full range of marketing activities, including digital marketing communications with measures to protect children and consumer privacy. It is a globally applicable self-regulatory framework, developed by experts from all industry sectors worldwide.
