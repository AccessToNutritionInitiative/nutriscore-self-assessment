Well-designed programs can support the improvement of employees’ health, increase their productivity, and reduce absenteeism. We recommend looking at the options available below and see what else you could do to ensure you're taking the best care of your employees.
Your practice can also positively influence your business partners along the supply chain to improve support to their employees. GAIN and the Consumer Goods Forum in 2019 started a Workforce Nutrition Alliance to:

1) advocate for an increased focus on Workforce Nutrition.
2) support and encourage employers to commit to - and implement - workforce nutrition programmes; and
3) monitor progress on these objectives.  

More information can be found here: <https://workforcenutrition.org/#/implementation-support>
