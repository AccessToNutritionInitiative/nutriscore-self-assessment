question_text: "Are you aware of the nutritional needs of the groups that you target?"
score: "0"
---

We recommend that you seek for information on the different needs in the population from your local health authority, government, civil society organisations or international agencies. This information can help you understand better how to reach vulnerable population groups with the marketing of your products and tailor your products to meet their needs.
