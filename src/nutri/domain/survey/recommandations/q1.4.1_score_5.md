question_text: "Do you know the exact nutrient composition of your products?"
score: "5"
---

Having knowledge of the nutrient composition of your products will help you to determine the levels of nutrients that are deemed healthy and can be consumed more often (e.g., protein, fibre, unsaturated fats), vs those for which limited consumption is recommended because of their negative effects on your consumers’ health when consumed in high quantities (e.g., salt, added sugar, or saturated fat). Understanding the nutrient composition of your products can help in product reformulation, but also in product labelling by being up front with consumers. Including nutrient composition on the front of back of your product informs your customers of the nutritional quality of your products and, if your products are healthier than those of your competitors’, – can help your products to stand out on the market.
Guidelines and informational documents on how to calculate the nutrient composition of your products can be found on the FAO’s website: <https://www.fao.org/fileadmin/templates/food_composition/documents/upload/y4705e00-1.pdf>
