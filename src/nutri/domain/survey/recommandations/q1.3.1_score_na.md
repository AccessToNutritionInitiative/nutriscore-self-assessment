Understanding which nutrition issues (i.e., underweight, stunting, wasting, micronutrient deficiencies, and/or overweight/obesity) are more severe in your country, and in any countries, you hope to export your products to, will help your company to formulate products that best fit consumers’ health and nutrition needs. Having knowledge of which specific population groups are affected will help guide you towards understanding which issues are most pressing and in need of focus.

To find out what nutrition issues are most pressing in your county or on a global scale please use reports based on sound science such as:
WHO Global Nutrtion Reports: <https://apps.who.int/nutrition/globalnutritionreport/en/index.html>
Global burden of disease study:  <https://www.thelancet.com/gbd>
2021 Global Nutrition report: <https://globalnutritionreport.org/>
Or follow your local governments advise.
